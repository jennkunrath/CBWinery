Menu Wunderkammern

Winemaker Chris Carlberg comments on his 2010 C.B. Estate Pinot Gris:

"One of the most expressive, elegant, white wines I have ever made. It has a beautiful drawn out intensity, reflecting so much of place and person. It finishes purely with a lingering reminder of its totality. It’s no simple “peck on the cheek” wine, it’s a full lip kiss. Unaffected, profoundly bright with fresh fruit sweetness and crisp acidity, this wine flirts as cheerfully with a summer salad as it compliments a more serious pairing with grilled chicken, pork cutlet or prawns."

Enjoy and share with us your latest adventure in food and wine!

Chris and Susanne
